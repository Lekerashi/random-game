How to Play

  1. Pick your starting station — GPS, search, or random.
  2. Roll the dice to pick a train line and direction.
  3. Head to the platform and board the next train.
  4. Roll again right away — ride that many stops.
  5. If you pass a transfer station, roll to decide: switch lines or stay on.
  6. Arrive at your stop, get off, and grab a drink!
  7. Head back to the station and go again!
